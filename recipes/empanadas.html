Empanadas Chilenas

Chilean empanadas bring a comforting flavour to the table.

Pino

  • 1 kg minced beef
  • 4 large onions chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves crushed
  • 2 TBSP cumin
  • 2 TBSP paprika
  • 2 g black pepper (1/2 TSP)
  • 12 g salt
  • 70 g raising
  • 2 TBSP flour

Cooking Instructions

  1. Pino: preferably, make it the evening before. (the house will smell lovely at bedtime) In a large pan add sunflower oil to fry the onions and garlic, until they are soft.
  2. Add the minced beef, salt and pepper, mix well until combined.
  3. Add the cumin and paprika, stir well then add the raisins.
